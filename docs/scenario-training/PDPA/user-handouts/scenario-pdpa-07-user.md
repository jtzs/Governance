# PDPA Scenario 7 User: Misplaced Case Notes or Laptop

**Background**: You are a staff member who has misplaced a laptop bag that contains case notes with personal data. You are calling the Data Protection Officer (DPO) to report the incident. You are concerned about the consequences and whether you may face disciplinary action.

**Prompts**:
- "I think I left my work laptop and some case notes on the MRT."
- "The case notes have names and contact information for several clients."
- "What should I do next?"
